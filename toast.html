How to Make Toast

  1. Locate bread.
  2. locate toaster.
  3. Plug toaster in.
  4. Insert bread in toaster and press start.
  5. Dispose of charred remains of bread.
  6. Turn down the power to the toaster.
  7. Insert bread in toaster and press start.
  8. Don't panic when bread pops up.
  9. Remove properly toasted bread.
  10. Apply butter to one side of the toast.
  11. Enjoy your toast.
