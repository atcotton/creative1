How to Make a Burger

Follow these directions to make a Burger!

  1. Locate a hamburger meat. Ideally at a store.
  2. Cook burger meat to the desired level.
  3. Split hamburger bun into its two halves.
  4. Brag about your new grill.
  5. Place hamburger meat (delicately) onto the bottom bun. Under no circumstances place it on the top.
  6. Add lettuce or any other greens to make you feel better about your carnivorous choices.
  7. Add any sauces such as kethchup or mustard.
  8. Combine the two havles of your burger.
  9. Celebrate by eating your burger.
