How to Make a Bowl of Cereal


Step 1: Gather Your Supplies.

The first step is to get the supplies needed for a bowl of cereal:

  1. Bowl
  2. Spoon
  3. Milk
  4. A box of cereal


Step 2: Get Bowl and Add Cereal

First get your bowl and place it on the counter top or table. Then add cereal.


Step 3: Add Milk.

Now it is time to add milk!

  1. Get milk
  2. Remove cap
  3. Pour onto cereal
  4. put cap back on


Step 4: Add Spoon and Enjoy!

Finally, add your spoon And enjoy your bowl of cereal!
